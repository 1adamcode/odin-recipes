Frito Pie

Description

Frito pie is a cool and convenient treat.

  • 1 large haas avocado
  • 3 fresh jalepenos chopped
  • 1/3 onion peeled and chopped
  • 2 roma tomatoes chopped
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro chopped
  • 4 tablespoons of sour cream
  • 2 limes
  • 1 18 oz can of chili
  • 1 18 oz can of cheese sauce
  • 1 bag of large Frito scoops

Steps

  1. Peel and pit avocado and mash up with a fork.
  2. Warm up chili on the stove for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Put cheese sauce into microwave safe bowl and warm up for 2 1/2 minutes. Stir halfway.
  4. Pour Frito scoops into a large bowl or plate.
  5. Pour chili over Frito chips and pour cheese sauce over Frito chips.
  6. Add the remaining ingredients as toppings to taste.
